The ASEAN Journal on Science & Technology for Development charges no fees whatsoever. This applies to both submission and publication of the article, as well as non-standard academic content, such as full-color figures and long article lengths—no costs are ever borne by authors.

This is made possible by the joint support of the ASEAN Committee on Science and Technology and Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology of the Republic of Indonesia, which enables us to operate without charging authors or readers.
