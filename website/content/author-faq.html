Does AJSTD charge any author fees?

No, we do not charge any fees whatsoever for publication in the journal. This is because AJSTD is fully supported by ASEAN COST.

Where is AJSTD indexed?

AJSTD is indexed in DOAJ and accredited by the Ministry of Research, Technology, and Higher Education of the Republic of Indonesia (Sinta Score: S2). The journal can also be found in various bibliographic databases such as Google Scholar and Microsoft Academic.

What is AJSTD's policy regarding copyright and author rights?

Because this is an open access journal, all articles are published with the aim of maximizing visibility, shareability, and the open transfer of knowledge. If published, your article will be licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA) license. This means that anyone will be free to copy, transform, or redistribute the article for any lawful purpose in any medium, provided they give appropriate attributionWe recommend the TASL method: the title of the article, its author(s), a link to the article on the AJSTD website, and a link to the license. to you as the original author(s) and AJSTD, link to the license, indicate if changes were made, and redistribute any derivative work under the same license.

You will retain the copyright of your article, without restrictions, even after publication. Only a non-exclusive license is granted to AJSTD to publish the article and identify itself as its original publisher, along with the commercial right to include the article in a hardcopy issue for sale to libraries and individuals.
